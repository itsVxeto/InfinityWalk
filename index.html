Who are our customers?

The customers have an average age range of 14 to 40 years old and of course they must have interest for gaming or IT.

What they want?

Basically, the customers would want an innovative and immersive product in the realms of gaming or IT. Next the focus is on providing high-quality gaming software, graphics cards, and peripherals to meet the demand for high-performance equipment.

What is our product?

Step into the future of gaming with Infinity Walk, an exclusive flooring system designed for gaming purposes and for personal use. Offers a versatile movement in every direction making a simple walk into something more immersive, suitable for gaming experience. The product can have a suit add-on designed to feel the experience in an exclusive way.

How is based our price?

The price is based on the cost of the materials, the fact that it’s an innovative and new product, the psychological effect and the way you buy the product (online or local shop).

How we are promoting?

To have a better result the promotion involves advertisement on many famous online stores (like Facebook, Instagram, Amazon and Google).

Where you can buy it?

Our product can be sold in local shop or if you are too lazy, you can rely on our transport, which ensure maximum safety for online purchase.
